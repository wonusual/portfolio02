Our new design philosophy
Opposites united

Bold for nature
We celebrate nature’s perfection and simplicity and
we pay homage to it in everything we do. We want to SHARE with you our vision in which NATURE
and MODERNITY can co-exist.
Tension for serenity
We want you to WITNESS that even in moments of TENSION,
you can find a sense of SERENITY.
We believe calm is the moment before the creative tension is unleashed.
Technology for life
We want to CREATE memorable and emotional moments in LIFE for you
by giving TECHNOLOGY a soul.
And we’re using that technology to put people first.
Joy for reason
We want you to FEEL that even through logical REASONING you can discover JOY.
We combine them to create a unique Kia experience.
Power to progress
Finally, we want to design vehicles that symbolize the power of speed.
REDEFINE the meaning of PROGRESS with INVENTIONS
and INNOVATIONS that inspire the way you think, move and live.
Opposites United is the inspiration for our designers here at Kia.
With that in mind, we strive to create the time and space to inspire you.
